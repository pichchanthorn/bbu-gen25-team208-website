About Us

BBU Gen25 – Web Programming Team 208

We are a dedicated group of Information Technology students from Build Bright University, Phnom Penh Campus. Our team represents Generation 25, Room 208, united by our passion for web development and design.

Who We Are

We are a team of four passionate IT students from Build Bright University, specializing in front-end web development and UI/UX design. Our diverse skills complement each other, allowing us to tackle various aspects of web development effectively.

As members of the Science and Technology and Foundation departments, we bring different perspectives and strengths to our collaborative work. Together, we create modern, responsive websites that not only look great but also provide excellent user experiences.

Our journey in web programming has taught us the importance of clean code, responsive design, and continuous learning. We embrace new technologies and methodologies to stay current in the ever-evolving field of web development.
